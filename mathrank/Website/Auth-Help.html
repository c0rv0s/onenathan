Authorization Help

What types of articles should I submit?

In order to authorize your article as a genuine article on the Knot network we need either some form of receipt of purchase or record from the original creator of the article. This will change depending on the particular article and may take several days to return a result. Some examples of documents that would work are as such:

  • Nike Sneakers: Letter of authenticity included with each pair of shoes.
  • Ray-bans: Either the letter of authenticity included in pakaging at purchase or a close up shot of the enscribed signature on the corner of the glasses.
  • Sports Memorabilia: Signature of original owner or signed letter from one of the notarized authorizers.

As the Knot service expands more detailed instructions will be added for specific items and categories of items. The receipt of purchase is always a good choice. This page will be updated as we enter further partnerships.
